01 About

Growing up between Cyprus and New York gave me something most marketers don't start with: an instinct for how stories land across cultures. I studied journalism at Hunter College, spent four years on the Dean's List, and got my first bylines in TV Guide Magazine before I even graduated. Writing for real people, not algorithms, is where it all started.

From there I moved into marketing, got my MSc in Marketing Communication from Stockholm University, and haven't looked back. I've built an influencer program from scratch at an AI startup, run social strategy across six global markets for a fintech company, and led content for a premium beauty brand. I've worked across three countries and way too many time zones.

What makes me different? I work end-to-end. I can write the brief, shoot and edit the content, analyze the data, and then build AI-powered tools to automate the boring parts. I don't just plan campaigns, I make them happen from start to finish. And I actually enjoy every part of it.
